PRIVACY POLICY

Your data. Your rights. Our commitment to transparency.

Last Updated: April 2026

Redcrown MMA ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data. By using our website or services, you consent to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, and email address provided via our waitlist form, contact form, or during enrollment.
  • Program Preferences: Your stated interests in specific training programs or time slots.
  • Health Information: Medical conditions or physical limitations you disclose to us for the purpose of safe training.
  • Usage Data: Standard web analytics data including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our website.
  • Communications: Records of messages, emails, or enquiries you send us.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To respond to enquiries and manage your membership or waitlist registration
  • To notify you when we launch or when schedule updates are available
  • To send relevant updates about programs, offers, and academy news (you can opt out at any time)
  • To ensure safe training by understanding any health conditions you share with us
  • To improve our website and services based on usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in operating our website or business (e.g. email providers), under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Where required by law, court order, or government authority
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of Redcrown MMA, our members, or the public

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. Waitlist data will be deleted within 12 months of our launch if you have not enrolled. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience and analyse site traffic. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect some features of our website. We do not use cookies for advertising purposes.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection law, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Opt out of marketing communications at any time by contacting us or clicking unsubscribe in any email.
  • Right to Complaint: Lodge a complaint with the relevant authority if you believe we have mishandled your data.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (such as Instagram). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies independently.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page regularly. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us
