MMA — Mixed Martial Arts

The complete fighter's system

Mixed Martial Arts is the most complete combat sports discipline in existence. It draws from striking, wrestling, clinch, and ground game — creating a fighter capable of handling any situation. At Redcrown, MMA training isn't a shortcut — it's a structured curriculum built on real foundations. BJJ — Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

The gentle art of control

BJJ is the chess of martial arts. It teaches you to control, submit, and survive using leverage and technique rather than raw power. Coach Aditya holds a verified BJJ Blue Belt — in a region where fake credentials are rampant, lineage matters. Classes cover positional training, submissions, escapes, and live rolling that builds real skill over time.

Muay Thai

The Art of Eight Limbs

Muay Thai is the most complete striking system in the world. Fists, feet, elbows, knees — eight weapons, total range control. Training develops devastating power, ring awareness, conditioning, and the kind of clinch work most fighters never understand. Whether you're a beginner or looking to sharpen your tools, Muay Thai is essential.

Boxing
