OUR STORY

ABOUT REDCROWN

Built by fighters. For everyone who wants to become one.

Why Redcrown Exists

The Delhi NCR martial arts scene has a problem. Walk into most "MMA gyms" and you'll find someone who watched a few YouTube compilations, bought some gloves and mats, and called themselves a coach. They teach what they've never done. They certify what they don't understand. And students pay — and get worse, not better.

Redcrown was built as the opposite of that.

"Most places here operate from basements and fabricate their credentials. We have trained fighters. We have competed. Your goal — we will build the most realistic version of it."

We bring verified BJJ lineage, actual competition experience, and a curriculum built from real martial arts study. When we say we know BJJ — we mean we have a traceable belt rank under a qualified instructor. When we say MMA — we mean we have been in the fight.

Noida Sector 18 doesn't need another gym. It needs a real academy. That's what we built.

OUR MISSION

To build the most realistic version of every student's ideal self — through disciplined, authentic martial arts training delivered by coaches who have lived it.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Old methods + new technology. Ancient martial principles refined through modern sports science. We respect what works, discard what doesn't, and build systems around individual goals.

OUR STANDARD

Every coach must be verified. Every technique must be tested. Every credential must be traceable. No performance art. No basement operations. No fake resumes.

YOUR GOAL

Whether it's competition, fitness, self-defence, or mental development — we don't have a one-size-fits-all plan. We build your blueprint, specifically.

THE NAME

What Does Redcrown Mean?

The crown represents mastery — earned, not given. Red is for the intensity of the pursuit, the fire of competition, the willingness to bleed for your craft. Redcrown is for those who don't just want to look like fighters — they want to become them. Enter the hunt.
