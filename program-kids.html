KIDS
Curriculum

What Kids Learn

01
BJJ FOUNDATIONS
Guard, mount, side control, escapes and the tap — learning positional safety from day one.
02
MUAY THAI BASICS
Jab, cross, teep and round kick — technique and control before any power or contact.
03
SAFE FALLING
Breakfalls, rolls and body awareness — protecting themselves before anything else is taught.
04
ANTI-BULLYING
De-escalation first. Practical self-defence second. Unshakeable confidence always.
05
DISCIPLINE & RESPECT
Bowing in, listening, supporting teammates — winning and losing with equal character.
06
BELT PROGRESSION
Gradings every 3–4 months — technique, attendance and attitude. All three matter equally.
