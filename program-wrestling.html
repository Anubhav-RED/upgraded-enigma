Curriculum

What You'll Learn

01
TAKEDOWN SYSTEMS
Double leg, single leg, high crotch — setup, fakes, timing, finish. Clean entries from every range.
02
GRECO-ROMAN CLINCH
Body lock, hip throw, suplex entries and cage-assisted throws. Upper-body dominance.
03
SPRAWL & BRAWL
Stopping takedowns, framing, shucking and immediately reversing into your own attack.
04
CAGE WRESTLING
Using the fence for control, takedowns, exits and positioning for ground-and-pound.
05
SCRAMBLE WORK
Re-shots, stand-ups, back takes and positional chess from uncomfortable, bad positions.
06
MMA INTEGRATION
Wrestling-to-striking transitions, ground-and-pound setup, ride-outs and cage pressure.
