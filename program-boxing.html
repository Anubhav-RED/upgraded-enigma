Curriculum

What You'll Learn

01
STANCE & GUARD
Orthodox/southpaw, weight distribution, chin protection, sight lines — the foundation everything else is built on.
02
JAB MASTERY
Range control, setting up power shots, the defensive jab, stick-and-move rhythm.
03
POWER COMBINATIONS
Cross, hook, uppercut mechanics. 2-punch to 6-punch combos with real power transfer.
04
FOOTWORK SYSTEMS
Advancing, retreating, circling, pivoting — moving to land, not just to escape.
05
DEFENSIVE MOVEMENT
Slipping, rolling, parrying, catching. Making opponents miss by inches, not feet.
06
COUNTER-PUNCHING
Reading the opponent, timing entries, turning pure defence into offence in one motion.
