Curriculum

What You'll Learn

01
POSITIONS & HIERARCHY
▶
Mount, back, side control, guard — dominance theory from day one. You always know where you are.
02
GUARD WORK
▶
Closed, half, butterfly, spider, De La Riva — attacking and surviving from the bottom.
03
SUBMISSIONS
▶
Chokes, armbars, leg locks, guillotines — finishing the fight through technique, not strength.
04
PASSING & SWEEPS
▶
Guard passing systems and reversal mechanics. Turn a bad position into a winning one.
05
BELT PROGRESSION
▶
Structured grading every 3–6 months — white through black belt. Clear goals at every stage.
06
COMPETITION PREP
▶
Points, advantages, submission-only rulesets and tournament tactics for those who want to compete.
