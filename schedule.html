Simple Process

How It Works.

01

Tell Your Friend

Let them know about Red Crown MMA — Noida's dedicated combat sports academy for BJJ, Muay Thai, Boxing, Wrestling, and MMA. Book them a free trial session so they can experience it first.

02

They Join on 6+ Months

Your friend signs up on a 6-month, half-yearly, or annual membership. Make sure they mention your name at sign-up — that's all that's needed.

03

Your Reward is Credited

After their second month's payment clears, 1 free month (or 2 if you're on annual) is automatically added to your membership. No forms. No chasing. Just train.

04

Repeat (Up to 3×)

There's no cap. Refer 5 people, get 5 free months. On annual and refer 5 people? That's 10 free months. Build the tribe, eliminate your training cost entirely.

Common Questions
Does my friend need to sign a long-term contract?
No contracts — Red Crown MMA memberships are prepaid terms, not lock-in contracts. Your friend simply commits to 6 months upfront to qualify.
Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?
No limit at all. Every qualifying referral earns you a free month. If you're on an annual plan, every qualifying referral earns you 2 free months. Refer enough people and your training is effectively free.
Can I refer family members?
Yes — family referrals are valid as long as the referred person is a new member who has not previously trained at Red Crown MMA Academy.
