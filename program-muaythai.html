Curriculum

What You'll Learn

01
STRIKING FUNDAMENTALS
Jab, cross, hook, uppercut — stance, guard, power generation and combination flow.
02
ELBOWS & KNEES
Horizontal, diagonal, spinning elbows. Knee strikes from distance and deep in the clinch.
03
TEEP & ROUND KICK
Push kick for range control. Round kick for power, damage and setting up combinations.
04
CLINCH CONTROL
Neck wrestling, framing, breaking — and the art of landing knees from the Thai clinch.
05
FOOTWORK & RHYTHM
Thai fighting stance, advance/retreat, circling, rhythm disruption and pressure.
06
PAD WORK & SPARRING
Coach-led round work, combination drilling, and progressive controlled sparring.
